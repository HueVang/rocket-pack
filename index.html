Rocketpack - Organic Climbing + Figure 8 LLC (Designer)

by Nate Flink

This backpack is designed for comfort and style. It's balanced for climbing,
providing durability over rocky terrain. Made in the USA.

The Rocketpack delivers the gear that a climber needs for a good day out!

This svelte backpack is designed for balance and comfort on every adventure.
It provides durability over rocky terrain, boulders, and mountains.
In a class by itself, the Rocketpack is compact, but beefy, with enough room to carry all the gear you need for your climbing day trips.


20% of proceeds go to the Access Fund!

Manufactured by Organic Climbing -

http://organicclimbing.com/
  • Rides higher on your back. Ergonomics for scrambling and climbing movement
  • Highest quality nylon fabrics for strength and light weight
  • Holds about 26 liters
  • Weighs 2 lbs
  • Bound and rolled seams
  • Sturdy, thick YKK coil zippers
  • Urethane coating. Moisture resistant
  • EVA foam shoulder straps. These aren't flimsy
  • Adjustable Sternum strap, removable
  • Load lifters to adjust fit and position
  • Expandable collar with drawstring for over stuffing the bag
  • Roomy lid compartment. Easy access for smaller items.
  • Foam waist belt. Removable and stows inside
  • Metal hook buckle with pull tab. Resistant to being crushed. Cinches down to compress your load (see below for the actual buckle)
  • Top loops allows gear and tools to be clipped along the top
  • Strong-point carry handle, strongly reinforced stitching
  • Three interior open topped pockets for securely stowing items
  • Zipper closure pocket inside the pack lid compartment for securely storing small items (Phone, Keys, etc.) Reduces possibility items will spill out

    when the lid compartment is opened
  • The production packs will feature deluxe finishing on all seems, and we plan to use a beautiful herringbone along the strap edges for comfort.
  • Designed for climbers by Figure 8 LLC
  • Manufactured in the USA by Organic Climbing
